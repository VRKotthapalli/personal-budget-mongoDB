About the Personal Budget Web Application

The Personal Budget web application is a user-friendly tool designed to help individuals manage their finances effectively. With this app, you can:

  • Track your expenses and income
  • Set and manage budget limits
  • Receive alerts when approaching budget limits
  • Gain insights into your spending habits
  • Plan and achieve financial goals

Take control of your finances with the Personal Budget app and start building a secure financial future.
